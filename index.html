Cupping Room,
Where every sip tells a story
Excel at making coffee, a cup at a time

Nestled in the heart of Pokhara, Cupping Room is a haven for coffee lovers and enthusiasts alike. Our mission is to provide a unique and immersive coffee experience that combines expertly crafted brews with a passion for education and community.

Step into our cozy shop and be greeted by the warm aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans. Our expert baristas are always happy to guide you through our extensive menu, featuring a range of specialty drinks made with love and care. From classic espresso to innovative cold brews, we have something for every taste and preference.

At Cupping Room, we're more than just a coffee shop – we're a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for coffee and a desire to learn. Join us for regular events, tastings, and workshops, and connect with fellow coffee enthusiasts from around the world.

Come and experience the Cupping Room difference for yourself. Visit us at our shop in Pokhara, Nepal, and let us welcome you to the world of specialty coffee.
